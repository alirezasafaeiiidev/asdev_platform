# Consulting Funnel UX Standard

## Required Funnel Stages

1. Positioning clarity
2. Service offer clarity
3. Proof and trust evidence
4. Lead capture CTA
5. Qualification intake

## Minimum UX Requirements

- Primary CTA visible above the fold
- Mobile-friendly lead form
- Friction-minimized progression to submission
- Accessible keyboard navigation

## KPI Baseline

- CTA click-through
- Form start rate
- Qualified lead rate
- Meeting booking conversion
